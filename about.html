Game Mechanics

  • Miles Driven is basically a counter how many cards you have clicked
  • Remaining matches tells you how many mathches is left
  • Current position is basically your accuracy percentage out of 43 cars
    • if you have good accuracy in matching the cards the more better your position is
    • ex. First Place is 100% accuracy and 43th is 0% accuracy
  • Current Lap is the count on how many games have you played
    • first lap is a practice lap then it would increment from there
  • Current Lap Time is your elapse time from that lap
  • Best Lap Time is the best lap time from you laps
